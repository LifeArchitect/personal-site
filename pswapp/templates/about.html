About Me

I'm a 3rd year undergraduate pursuing a direct masters in Computer Science & Business from UC Berkeley and Nanyang Technological University (NTU), under the Renaissance Engineering Programme. My passion lies in using cutting-edge technology to empower organizations and people in the areas of predictive modeling and human potential.

As a Full Stack Machine Learning Developer, I have built chatbots, blockchain web apps and predictive modeling algorithms during my work with Ramco Systems, DataRobot and my Personal Projects, including graduating from Udacity's Deep Learning Nanodegree Foundation Program. As an Entrepreneur, I have experience in product sales, digital marketing and management consulting during my work with Awaken Group, ICON, Street Smart University, and founded my previous start up, Pursue Your Passion, to provide career coaching for youths.
